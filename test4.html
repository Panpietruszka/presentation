Security

No compromises.

Security starts with Intel Lunar Lake and extends to the Samsung Knox architecture. This deep integration of hardware and software along with automatic software updates helps keep Galaxy Book stable and protected for the long term. The security architecture also powers features such as Fingerprint ID, SmartThings Find, and advanced defenses that protect against viruses and malware.

Fingerprint ID. Unlock your Galaxy Book, sign in to apps, and make secure payments with your fingertip. The Samsung Knox security chip keeps your fingerprint data safe.

SmartThings Find. Locate your misplaced Galaxy Book and remotely lock or erase it if needed.

FileVault. Encrypt and protect your files and data without having to think about it.
